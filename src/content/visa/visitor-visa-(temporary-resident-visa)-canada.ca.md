Visitor visa (temporary resident visa) A visitor visa (also called a temporary resident visa) is an official document that we stick in your passport. It shows that you meet the requirements needed to travel to Canada. Some citizens of Indonesia and Malaysia are now eligible to apply for an eTA As of May 26, 2026, citizens of Indonesia and Malaysia who meet certain requirements may be eligible to apply for an eTA instead of a visitor visa, to travel to Canada by air. If you already have a valid visitor visa, you can use that to travel to Canada until it expires. Find out what you need to travel to Canada.
