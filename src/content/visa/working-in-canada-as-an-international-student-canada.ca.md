Changes to co-op work permit requirements for post-secondary international students As of April 1, 2026, post-secondary international students don’t need a co-op work permit for student work placements such as co-op placements or internships. Co-op work permit holders can keep using their permit for its entire validity. Find out if you’re eligible for a student work placement.
