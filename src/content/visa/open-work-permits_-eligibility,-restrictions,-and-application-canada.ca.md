Open work permits There are 2 types of work permits: open work permits and employer-specific work permits. You can get an open work permit in certain situations. Open work permits aren’t job-specific, so if you have one, your employer doesn’t need to apply for a labour market impact assessment submit an offer of employment pay the employer compliance fee Who can apply Most people applying from outside Canada can’t get an open work permit. Most open work permit applicants must apply in Canada. You can apply for an open work permit only under specific situations.
