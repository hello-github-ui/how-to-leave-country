Find out what type of work permit you need Get the right instructions to apply. Answer a few questions to find out: if you need a work permit or if you’re exempt if you need an open or employer-specific work permit if you need special instructions to apply Find out what type of work permit you need Types of work permits There are 2 types of work permits. The type you can apply for depends on your eligibility. Employer-specific work permit Most common You need a job offer. Your employer may need a labour market impact assessment (LMIA). Open work permit For special circumstances You don’t need a job offer. You can work for most compliant employers in Canada. You’re only eligible in certain situations.
