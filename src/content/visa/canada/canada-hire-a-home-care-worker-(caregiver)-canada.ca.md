Hire a home care worker (caregiver) There are a few ways you can hire a home care worker (caregiver). Your options depend on whether you’re inside or outside Quebec where the home care worker (caregiver) will work The information on this page applies to employers looking to hire a home child care provider (National Occupation Classification 44100) home support worker (National Occupation Classification 44101) If you need to hire a worker in another occupation, follow the process to hire a foreign worker. On this page Hire a worker who’s in Canada Hire a worker who’s outside Canada Hire a worker as an employer in Quebec Hire a worker who’s in Canada If you’re an employer (outside Quebec), you can hire a home care worker through one of the programs below. Home Care Worker Immigration Pilots: Workers in Canada stream: Option 1 of 4 Closed The Home Care Worker Immigration pilots: Workers in Canada stream allows Canadian employers (outside Quebec) to hire qualified home care workers already in Canada. How to hire under this pilot Home Child Care Provider and Home Support Worker pilots: Option 2 of 4 Closed to new applicants on June 17, 2024 You can only hire workers through these pilots if either the worker already has an open work permit without restrictions that would prevent them from working in child care or health services the worker already has a work permit under one of the pilots Temporary Foreign Worker Program: Option 3 of 4 You can only hire workers through this program if they meet these 2 conditions: the worker either already has a work or study permit is allowed to work in Canada without a work permit (this doesn’t apply to business visitors) will work in Quebec you can get a positive Labour Market Impact Assessment (LMIA) and have your candidate apply for a new LMIA-based work permit Hire through the Temporary Foreign Worker International Mobility Program (open work permits other than the pilots): Option 4 of 4 You can only hire workers under this program if the worker already has an open work permit without restrictions that would prevent them from working in child care or health services Hire a worker who’s outside Canada If you’re an employer (outside Quebec), you can hire a home care worker through one of the programs below. Home Care Worker Immigration Pilots: Applicants not working in Canada stream: Option 1 of 4 Closed How to hire under this pilot (once it opens) Home Child Care Provider and Home Support Worker pilots: Option 2 of 4 Closed to new applicants on June 17, 2024 You can only hire workers through these pilots if either you already submitted a job offer to a worker, and they submitted their permanent residence application before the program closed the worker already has an open work permit without restrictions that would prevent them from working in child care or health services Temporary Foreign Worker Program: Option 3 of 4 You can only hire through this program if the worker will work in Quebec. Hire through the Temporary Foreign Worker Program. Hire a worker as an employer in Quebec If you’re an employer in Quebec, you can hire a home care worker who’s outside or in Canada through the Temporary Foreign Worker Program.
