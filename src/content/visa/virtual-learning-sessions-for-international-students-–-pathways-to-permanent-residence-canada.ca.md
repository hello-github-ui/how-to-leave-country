Virtual learning sessions for international students – Pathways to permanent residence We’re offering a virtual questions and answers series for international students who want to stay in Canada after they graduate. Learn about pathways to permanent residence in Canada Each 1-hour session will allow international students to ask questions about future pathways to permanent residence in Canada. Browse and register To prepare for these sessions, students should watch the related video before they register. Watch the introduction video. Register for: sessions in English sessions in French Students can register for one or more sessions. No case-specific questions will be answered during these sessions. Page details 2025-02-13
