Rural and Francophone Community Immigration pilots These pilots offer permanent residence to skilled candidates who want to work and live in one of 18 selected Canadian communities. Employers can contact their community to get designated and hire in priority sectors. Apply for permanent residence Find out if you’re eligible, get a job offer, apply under a pilot program, and get a work permit while you wait.
