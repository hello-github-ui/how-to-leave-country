Who can work without a work permit Most foreign nationals need a work permit to work in Canada. Some workers, such as business visitors, foreign representatives and military personnel, are exempt. On this page Check if you need a work permit Jobs that may not need a work permit Check if you need a visa to enter Canada Check if you need a work permit You may not need a work permit if your job is exempt or if you meet the requirements for the exemption Check if you need a work permit. Jobs that may not need a work permit You may not need a work permit if you’re working in an occupation included in the list below. However, even if your occupation is listed, you may still need to meet certain requirements to be eligible for a work permit exemption.
